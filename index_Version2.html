About Us

AL-MANAF'U INTERNATIONAL ACADEMY is committed to providing high-quality, holistic education where academic excellence is combined with moral development and leadership training.

Our Mission

To empower every student with knowledge, integrity and skills to thrive in a diverse world.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of learning recognized for exceptional teaching and community engagement.

Our Values

Respect • Excellence • Compassion • Responsibility • Innovation

Programs

We offer a continuum of learning from primary through secondary levels with extra-curricular opportunities.

Early Years

Play-based learning that builds curiosity and foundational skills.

Primary School

Strong literacy and numeracy with integrated science and arts.

Secondary School

Preparation for national and international examinations with career guidance.

STEM & ICT

Hands-on labs and digital literacy to prepare students for the future.

Sports & Clubs

Physical education, music, drama and leadership clubs.

Boarding

Safe, nurturing boarding environment for out-of-town students.
